About The Dentist

Meet Dr. Pooja

Dr. Pooja is a leading practitioner in cosmetic and restorative dentistry based in Boston. Committed to biological dentistry and modern technical diagnostics, she combines standard clinical wisdom with computer-guided dental modeling.

Our clinic is equipped with the latest 3D digital scanners and intraoral cameras, allowing us to map your dental structure with microscopic precision. Every treatment plan is tailored to the unique anatomy of your teeth, ensuring maximum comfort and lasting results.

Advanced 3D Imaging: Full mouth micro-scans for absolute diagnostic precision.
Minimally Invasive: Preserving natural tooth structure through laser and micro-dentistry.
